Yahtzee Dice Roller

Instructions:

Welcome to the Yahtzee Dice Roller! This tool allows you to simulate rolling five dice for the Yahtzee game.

To roll the dice, simply click the "Roll Dice" button below. The values of the dice will be displayed both graphically and in the table below.

If you need to roll the dice again, you can also press the Enter key after the page loads or after clicking the button.
